    Comfortable with Carbs

    Just like protein to much carbohydrates can be detremental however to little can be too. Your body loves carbs and has a how biological system just to deal with them. Your body uses carbs to create glucose and is responsible for many functions in the body. One of these functions is a energy source. Your body stores glucose as glycogen and stores this within the muscles and liver to use mainly for quick bursts of energy, when exercising, and hormone control. Any excess carbs will be converted in to fat which can be harmful since to much fat can cause health problems. In some cases people can get genetic defects where their body makes to much fat and ends up not producing enough hormones and the opposite could happen where the body produces to many hormones and does not convert the necessary carbs to fat.

    Fatty Friends

    Fats are too very important for your body. Even though your body can convert glucose into fat it is mainly only used for energy. Enable to utilize the full functionality of fats you will need to eat Nutritious fatty foods such as avocardos, dark chocolate, almonds and many more. these fats help can also provide energy mainly for long-durations and small activities such as walking and typing. Fats can also help lubricate joints and cells, acts as an insulater for the body and helps produce important hormones.

    Fruits and Vegg

    Fruits and vegtables are not surprisingly very important for your diet. It provides many vitamins and minerals which your body uses to help boost the immune system, support normal growth and development and increases cell productivity. Fruits and veg also are high in dietery fibre which improves gut health which can lead the body to digest food more easily and effectivly. I have seen many people not enjoy fruits and vegtables or get put off by people saying they need to each every fruit and veg on the planet. Luckily this is not true. There is a reason why there is a saying that goes and apple a day keeps the doctor way. All you really need is one type of fruit and one type of vegtable that you like everyday and you would be fine. However a little bit or varity can go a long way, so don't be affraid to experiments and find new fruits and vegtables that you enjoy.

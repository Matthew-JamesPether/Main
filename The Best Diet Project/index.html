    • What is it?


    The best diet should be a diet that is enjoyable and suits your everyday needs. This will enable your diet to become a lifestyle and enhance all areas in life. Your diet should take into account your genetics, your goals and the options that are avalible to you.

    How to start?


    I wish I could give you a set diet and tell you to follow it for a couple months and you will reach your goal. Unfortunately for the majorty of people this will not work. If you want to try and find a diet that works for you, here are some tips that I have used that help me:


    1. Find out what your goals ares. Depending on your goals in life it will influence how your diet is structured. Someone that is training to be a professional swimmer will have a different diet to someone that wants to be a professional weightlifter or someone that just wants to exercise for fun.
    2. Find out how your genetics work. Everyone is born different and requires and different set of nutrients to function a full capacity. People that end up with iron deficiency typically need to eat iron-rich food such as red meats. However if your goal is to be vegetarian or vegan you would have to find another source iron-rich food such as spinach or beans. This is but one of many examples on how your genetics and goals influence your diet. There are many ways to find out your genetics one of the ways is through trial and error however this may take awhile. A quicker way is to get a full blood check buy a respectable dietian however this is a bit more expensive.
    3. Find out whats avalible to you. Not everyone has the options to afford anything they want. My best advice to you is to create a spending budget that you can afford and find foods in your area that fit within the budget.
    4. Finding out what you enjoy. There is no point in creating a diet that you end up hating. Your diet should be enjoyable and fulling. Leading you wanting to eat it everyday.
    5. Not be affaid to have a cheat meal once or twice a week. Go out and eat with friends. As long as it is within reason, you carry on with your diet and you are consistent you should be fine.
    6. Calorie counting is the most important part in creating a personal diet. Once you know your calorie intake you can asign your food groups to a calorie amount to prevent any unwanted weight loss or weight gain when creating your best diet.
